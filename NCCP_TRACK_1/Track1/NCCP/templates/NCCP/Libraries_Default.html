Libraries

The Nevada Climate Change Project has compiled a collection of resources related to the general topic of climate change, climate research, and project development activities. These resources, though available elsewhere on this site and the Internet, are organized centrally in the project library. This collection includes videos, links to related content, publications, and any other resources that may be of use or interest to the public, researchers, or organizations interested in the project and climate change.

The content made available as a part of the Nevada Climate Change Project is available for general public use. This use is subject to the restriction that proper credit and / or citations are given to the authors of the content. The content of other non-project resources is available under the specific terms of the originating site, location, and / or author(s). These resources include, for example, links to other websites, publications hosted on other sites, and graphics shown on other sites.
